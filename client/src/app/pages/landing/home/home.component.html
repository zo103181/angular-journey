Angular Journey

This is a personal project of mine that allows you to track your fill-ups, fuel consumption, fuel costs. Utilizing the Geolocation API we attempt to locate fuel stations in your current area. If we can't determine the station you can help us by filling in the location section of your fuel logs.

Project Details

I want to work more with Angular and figured this application would be a perfect way to get started. My goal is to document this journey on Medium, share my experiences, and get input and direction from my fellow peers. My current plan is to tackle a small part of the application every two weeks and have a story written / published on or before that Sunday. I hope you will join me on this journey!
